Children's Ministry

The Goal of Children’s Ministry
The goal of the children's ministry is to aid parents at all times in ministering the basic ethics and principles of faith for children by teaching them the Word of God in such a way that it is understandable and appropriate to their age group.
Sunday Morning
Currently, the children's ministry is represented by two distinct and separate branches: Sunday School and Children's Church. Sunday School meets at 9:30 am every Sunday, and is currently divided into two classes.
Vision
We consider religious education for your child incredibly important for developing his or her ethics foundation. We don’t believe that kids ministry should be a babysitting service, but a platform for developing foundations that will set our kids up for God.
