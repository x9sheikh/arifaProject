Paul’s Vision in His Own Words

When the message of God gets distorted by people around you, it may be finally time to come to the church and witness the wisdom and grace of it yourself. I invite each of you to one of my sermons this or next week!

As a pastor I always feel like talking to people is what I should do the most.

Actually, you don’t even imagine how many people need spiritual guidance in their life!

As a Christian missionary, I know a thing or two about such things as:

  • The Scripture
  • Gospels
  • Guilt
  • Hatred & Love
  • Ethics

I sincerely hope that by attending our church, listening to what good I have to say and becoming a part of our mutually acceptive and supportive, vibrant and diverse community you will find a peace of mind!
